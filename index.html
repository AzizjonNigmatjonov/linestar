      About us

      Founded by a former trucker, Lonestar Logistics is a freight company that places its drivers at the forefront of its operations. We’re a company that’s constantly evolving its ways to make drivers feel like valued family members. Our team is vast, experienced, and are fully supported on and off the road in North America.

      We provide truckers with a state of the art fleet using vehicles such as the latest Freightliner, Volvos, Kenworth, or International vehicles before their commercial release. Plus, we have a track record for ensuring timely deliveries and surpassing our customer expectations.

      Most importantly, we devote ourselves to building respectful relationships for our drivers and logistics team members. We believe we only can succeed if we operate according to their needs.
