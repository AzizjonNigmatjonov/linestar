ABOUT US

Family focused and solution orientated

At Linestar, we pride ourselves on our lifetime accessibility. We’re here to help you with your transportation needs and answer any query.

  • Innovative logistic company

    Linestar logistics is an innovative carrier company devoted to ensuring rapid delivery times for customers in America. We supply our high caliber truckers with state-of-the-art fleet that changes on a 24 month basis. All are equipped with transponders and Elogs to ensure your freight is delivered with as few time constraints as possible.

  • Opportunities for team members

    Transparency is at the heart of our company, and our drivers are more than just a number. We provide a wealth of opportunities for sustainable career growth for each member. Just ask our founder; he was a trucker himself and knows first hand the frustrations and feats many truckers face.

  • Bonuses for our team

    It's for this reason; he's regularly providing insights and ways to help make Linestar team members feel appreciated. At present, truckers with us are entitled to advances, direct deposits, awards, vacation pay, referral programs, holiday pay, bonuses, and more.

  • 24/7 support and safety

    Plus, drivers are never left in the dark, whether they're behind the wheel or at home. At any time they need, they have a 24/7 logistics team to help with their reloads, deliveries, and safety.

  • Our team is our secret formula

    This modern approach towards growth is part of the reason why most of our drivers have stayed with us from our birth till today. Our consistent focus and support towards our team is the secret formula to operating our services efficiently. We believe that we can only surpass our customers' needs by building flourishing relationships with our employees.
